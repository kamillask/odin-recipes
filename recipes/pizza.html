Pizza Recipe

Classic recipe for Brooklyn style pizza dough, use whatever toppings you'd like.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Add warm water to a bowl, add yeast, let sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Add cold water and salt, stir in flour 1 cup at a time.
  3. Knead dough on floured surface til smooth, ~10 minutes.
  4. Cover dough and put in fridge overnight.
  5. Roll out dough, add toppings, and cook til bottom is light brown.
