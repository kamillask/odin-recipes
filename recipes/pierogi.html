Pierogi Recipe

This is a recipe for traditional Polish pierogi.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbs butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cups mashed potatoes
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp white pepper

Steps

  1. Gather your ingrdients
  2. Melt butter in skillet, sweat onions
  3. Add in mashed potatoes, salt and papper
  4. Put filling in dough, boil until floating
